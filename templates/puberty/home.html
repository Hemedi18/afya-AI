Puberty Information

What is Puberty?

Puberty is the process of physical changes through which a child's body matures into an adult body capable of sexual reproduction. It is initiated by hormonal signals from the brain to the gonads: the ovaries in a girl, the testes in a boy.

Common Signs of Puberty
  • Growth spurt (height and weight)
  • Development of breasts (girls)
  • Growth of facial, pubic, and underarm hair
  • Voice changes (boys)
  • Start of menstruation (girls)
  • Acne and skin changes
  • Emotional changes
Tips for a Healthy Puberty
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Exercise regularly
  • Maintain good hygiene
  • Get enough sleep
  • Talk to trusted adults about changes
  • Ask questions and seek support
When to See a Doctor
  • No signs of puberty by age 14 (boys) or 13 (girls)
  • Very early or very late puberty
  • Severe emotional or physical symptoms
