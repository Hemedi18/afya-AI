About AI Health Assistant

AI Health Assistant is a cutting-edge platform designed to provide personalized health insights and support. Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health through the power of artificial intelligence.

Our Features

  • Symptom Checker: Get instant insights into your symptoms and potential causes.
  • Menstrual Health Tracking: Log your menstrual cycle and receive personalized predictions and insights.
  • Pregnancy Support: Track your pregnancy journey and get tailored advice and information.
  • AI-Powered Chat: Ask our AI assistant any health-related questions and receive accurate, evidence-based answers.

Our Team

Our team is composed of healthcare professionals, data scientists, and software engineers who are passionate about improving health outcomes through technology.
